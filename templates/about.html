About Project

In the era of rapid urbanization, traffic congestion has emerged as a critical challenge for city planners and commuters alike. Traditional traffic management systems often rely on static methods that fail to adapt to real-time conditions, leading to inefficient traffic flow and increased travel times. This project proposes a smart traffic management solution that employs machine learning techniques to predict traffic levels effectively. By categorizing traffic conditions into Low, Normal, High, and Heavy, the system aims to provide timely insights to traffic management authorities and commuters
