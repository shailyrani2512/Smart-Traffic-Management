Motive

The motivation behind this project stems from the urgent need to address the growing traffic congestion in urban areas, which results in increased travel times, higher fuel consumption, and elevated pollution levels. As cities expand and populations grow, traditional traffic management strategies become insufficient. The project aims to harness the power of machine learning to develop a predictive traffic management system that offers real-time traffic conditions.
